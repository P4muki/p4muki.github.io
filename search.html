Search Helper

Type what you're looking for and get one-click links to search it on both FINN.no and Facebook Marketplace, so you can compare results yourself.

// heads up: this opens FINN.no and Facebook Marketplace in new tabs with your search pre-filled — it doesn't pull in an AI comparison of listings. Neither site offers a public search API, and Facebook actively blocks automated scraping of Marketplace, so a real backend would be needed to fetch and rank results automatically. This gets you both searches side by side in one click instead.
